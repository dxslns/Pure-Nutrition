Olive Oil

Vegetable Oil

85/100

Caloric content

About 884 kcal per 100g. Olive oil is calorie-dense due to its high fat content.

9/10

Fat Type

Rich in monounsaturated fats, particularly oleic acid, which has numerous health benefits. Contains some polyunsaturated fats.

9/10

Omega-6 to Omega-3 Ratio

Typically, olive oil has a favorable omega-6 to omega-3 ratio, making it heart-healthy.

8/10

Smoke Point

Extra virgin olive oil: ~190-220°C. Refined olive oil: ~240°C. Ideal for low to medium heat cooking.

7/10

Trans Fats

Contains no trans fats when unrefined.

10/10

Naturalness / Purity

Extra virgin olive oil is the least processed and retains most of its nutrients. Refined olive oil has undergone more processing.

9/10

Vitamins

Rich in vitamins E and K, which are beneficial for skin, bone health, and antioxidant protection.

8/10
