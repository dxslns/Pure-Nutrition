Beef

Meat

Rating S

Caloric content

Approximately 250 calories per 100 grams (depending on the cut of meat and cooking method)

Energy value

High energy value, primarily from proteins and fats

Glycemic index

0 (beef contains no carbohydrates, so it does not affect blood sugar levels)

Micronutrients

Beef is rich in important micronutrients such as iron, zinc, and selenium

Vitamins

High in B vitamins, especially B12, B6, and niacin (B3)

Dietary fiber

Contains no dietary fiber, as it is an animal product

Allergens

Beef can cause allergic reactions in some individuals, but this is quite rare. The main allergens in meat may be related to processing or additives
