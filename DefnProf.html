Deficiency & Excess

Understand what happens when nutrients are lacking or too abundant, and how to maintain optimal levels.

Understanding Nutrient Balance

Your body needs a delicate balance of nutrients to function optimally. Both deficiencies and excesses can lead to health issues, impacting metabolism, immunity, cognitive function, and long-term wellness.

Common Nutrient Deficiencies

Common deficiencies include iron (anemia), vitamin D (weak bones, fatigue), B12 (neurological symptoms), iodine (thyroid issues), and magnesium (cramps, anxiety). These often arise due to poor diet, absorption issues, or increased needs.

Symptoms of Excess

Overconsumption, often from supplements, can be harmful. Too much vitamin A may cause toxicity, excess iron can damage organs, and high calcium intake can lead to kidney stones. Balance is key & more is not always better.

Maintaining Optimal Levels

Eating a varied, whole-food diet is the most effective way to meet nutrient needs. Regular blood work, awareness of symptoms, and responsible supplementation can help you stay in a safe, effective range.
